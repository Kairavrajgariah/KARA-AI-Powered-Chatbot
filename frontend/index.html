About Sattva Restaurant Project

Sattva Restaurant is a demonstration of a modern, AI-powered food ordering platform designed to streamline the restaurant experience for both customers and staff. This project integrates advanced conversational AI with robust backend technology to deliver a seamless, interactive, and efficient ordering process.

  • Frontend: Responsive, modern UI using HTML5 & CSS3 (glassmorphism, mobile-friendly design)
  • Backend: FastAPI (Python) for scalable, high-performance APIs
  • Conversational AI: Dialogflow Messenger (Google Cloud) for natural language customer interaction
  • Database: Cloud-hosted MySQL for secure, reliable data management

Key features include real-time chatbot ordering, menu browsing, order tracking, and cloud deployment. Sattva Restaurant exemplifies the future of digital dining through technology-driven hospitality.
